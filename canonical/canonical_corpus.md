### SLIDE 12 HANDLING DISAGREEMENT AND MISINFORMATION
- Canonical ID: `canon_be6ec8586c31`
- Status: `lightly_transformed`
- Sources: Speaker Script Guide.pdf

When conversations about law or politics become contentious or misinformation arises, ambassadors should respond with curiosity, empathy, and composure. Asking about the source of information, identifying underlying concerns, and maintaining calm helps keep discussions productive and models civil discourse. The goal is not immediate persuasion, but to plant seeds and demonstrate respectful engagement.
